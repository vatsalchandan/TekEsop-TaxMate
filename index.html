Assumptions for Stock Grant Tax Calculations:

  • Single Grant Calculation: All calculations are based on one stock grant.
  • Income Tax Slab: A flat tax rate of 30% is assumed for income tax purposes.
  • Surcharge: No surcharge is considered in these calculations.
  • Perquisite Tax: The company collects perquisite taxes at a rate of 42.74%.(Can be changed in input box)
  • Stock Holding Period:
    • Unlisted stock is considered short-term if sold within 24 months of acquisition.
    • Short-term capital gains are taxed at a rate of 30%.
    • Long-term capital gains (post-2024 budget) are taxed at a rate of 12.5%.
  • Exercise Status: If the stock is classified as long-term, it has already been exercised.
  • Administrative Fees: These fees are listed in the Carta system for each stock grant.
  • Other Fees: These fees are mentioned in Carta:
    • One line above the "Cost to Exercise."
    • One line below the "Withholding (Perquisites Taxes)."
  • Exercised Stocks: If the stock has already been exercised, perquisite taxes do not apply. Only capital gains taxes will be considered.
